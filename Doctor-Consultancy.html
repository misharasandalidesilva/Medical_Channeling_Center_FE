SPECIALITY OVERVIEW

Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive heart care services including diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart diseases. Our experienced cardiologists use the latest technology to deliver high-quality cardiac care.

Dermatology

Our dermatology department offers comprehensive care for conditions affecting the skin, hair, and nails. Our dermatologists are experienced in diagnosing and treating various skin conditions, from common concerns like acne to more complex diseases.

Psychiatry

Our psychiatry department is dedicated to mental health care and treatment. Our psychiatrists provide compassionate care for a range of mental health conditions, including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and more.

Neurology

Our neurology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and nervous system. Our team of neurologists is dedicated to providing advanced care for complex neurological conditions.

Orthopedics

Experience optimal musculoskeletal care with our Orthopaedic doctors. From long term joint pains to sports injuries, our team is equipped to provide advanced orthopaedic treatments to help you gain mobility to enhance your overall quality of life.

Pediatrics

Our pediatrics department offers comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatricians are committed to providing preventive care, treating illnesses, and supporting the healthy development of children.

Endocrinology

Our endocrinology department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders related to hormones and the endocrine system. Our endocrinologists provide comprehensive care for conditions like diabetes, thyroid disorders, and metabolic conditions.

ENT Specialist

Our ENT department provides specialized care for ear, nose, and throat conditions. Our specialists are experienced in diagnosing and treating a wide range of ENT disorders, from common issues like sinusitis to more complex conditions.

General Surgery

Our general surgery department offers a wide range of surgical procedures. Our surgeons are skilled in both traditional and minimally invasive techniques, ensuring the best outcomes for our patients.

Physician

Our physicians are dedicated to providing comprehensive primary care. They focus on preventive care, management of chronic diseases, and coordination of care for patients with complex medical needs.

Health and Lifestyle Management

Our Health and Lifestyle Management department focuses on promoting overall wellness through lifestyle modifications. Our specialists provide guidance on nutrition, exercise, stress management, and other lifestyle factors to improve health outcomes.

Gastroenterology

Our gastroenterology department provides comprehensive care for digestive disorders. Our specialists are experienced in diagnosing and treating diseases affecting the gastrointestinal tract and liver.

Obstetrics and Gynaecology

Our obstetrics and gynaecology department provides comprehensive care for women throughout all stages of life. From routine check-ups to specialized treatments, our team is dedicated to women's health and well-being.

Oncology

Our oncology department offers comprehensive cancer care, including screening, diagnosis, treatment, and survivorship support. Our multidisciplinary team works together to provide personalized treatment plans for each patient.
